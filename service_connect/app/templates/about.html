About Service Connect

Service Connect is a digital platform designed to help local community members report service delivery issues such as water leaks, electricity problems, and potholes.

Our goal is to make communication between citizens and municipalities simple, fast, and effective. Users can easily report issues, track progress, and receive updates in real time.

Our Mission

To improve service delivery by providing a user-friendly platform that ensures transparency, efficiency, and accountability.

What We Offer

  • Easy issue reporting
  • Real-time feedback
  • Progress tracking
  • Direct communication with municipalities
